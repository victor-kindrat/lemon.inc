The best staffing

Since 2015, we are proud to say that we have a proven record in providing leading firms and candidates with professional expertise, in addressing their specialist employment needs. Our combined knowledge attained from over years of experience in recruitment; and our deep understanding of both our candidates’ and clients’ technical, cultural, personal and professional needs, assures you of efficient, effective and personalised service.
When it comes to staffing – particularly, attracting and selecting the right candidates, recruiting, interviewing, sifting through and reading hordes of applicants; we are proud to say that Lemon Inc comes highly recommended.
We value our clients, their time and money spent on recruitment, in return we offer our undivided attention to their needs and requirements regarding the selection of your ideal candidate.

Our Aim

Our aim is to effectively meet the growing need and market expectation for a more personal outcome based recruitment service. Passionate about the service we offer, we are professional in our approach, thus providing our clients with our undivided attention throughout the service, with a very clear and determined commitment to changing the perceptions the market has of the recruitment industry as a whole including how they operate and the value they add to the process of Recruitment Services.
It is our aim to understand the business and culture of each client, communication provided to our clients with appropriate CV’s of suitable candidates.
